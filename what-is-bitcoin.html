What is bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital currency that allows you to send payments over the Internet. Why is bitcoin better for betting?
It's a decentralized currency. The bitcoin network isn't controlled, or responsible to any government or bank.
Bitcoin addresses can be setup in seconds and one has already been created for you when you joined Bitbrawlers!
It's faster and easier to transfer funds with bitcoin and the fees are lower than traditional bank transfers. Likewise, it's much easier to gamble with bitcoin in countries that restrict gambling deposits.
Bitcoin addresses are anonymous. No personal information is attached to your bitcoin address, and your Bitbrawlers account is completely anonymous. Certain bitcoin currency exchanges may ask for personal information, but it is still possible to exchange money anonymously and will be discussed later in this guide.
